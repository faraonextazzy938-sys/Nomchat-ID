Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how Nomchat ID collects, uses, and protects your personal data. By using our platform, you agree to these terms.

1. What we collect

  • Email address — used for authentication via one-time codes
  • Username — chosen by you during registration
  • Avatar — selected from our preset list
  • Login timestamps — when you last signed in
  • IP address — used for rate limiting and security

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal data.

2. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you across Nomchat ID connected apps
  • To display your profile in connected applications
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To send verification codes to your email

3. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • nc_consent — stores your cookie consent choice (365 days)
  • nc_functional — enables core functionality like staying signed in (365 days)
  • nc_analytics — anonymous usage analytics (365 days, only if accepted)
  • session — server-side session cookie for authentication (session)

You can decline non-essential cookies via the banner shown on first visit.

4. Data sharing

We share your username, email, and avatar with connected applications (like AI Chat Pro) when you authorize them via Nomchat ID. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Data retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from your profile page, which permanently removes all your data.

6. Bans

Accounts that violate our Terms of Service may be banned by administrators or developers. Banned accounts cannot sign in. The ban reason is stored in our database and visible to you on your profile.

7. Your rights

  • Access your data — visible on your profile page
  • Delete your account — available in profile settings
  • Update your username and avatar — available in profile
  • Withdraw cookie consent — clear cookies in your browser

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@nomchat.id
