Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

We use cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. You can decline non-essential cookies via the banner on first visit.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit, such as your login session or language preference.

Cookies we use

Name Type Duration Purpose
session Required Session Keeps you signed in. Set by the Flask server after successful authentication.
nc_consent Required 365 days Stores your cookie consent choice (accepted or declined).
nc_functional Functional 365 days Enables functional features like language preference. Set only if you accept cookies.
nc_analytics Analytics 365 days Anonymous usage analytics to improve the platform. Set only if you accept cookies.

Cookie types explained

Required cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. Without them, you cannot sign in or use core features.

Functional cookies

These cookies remember your preferences (like language) to provide a better experience. They are set only if you accept cookies via the consent banner.

Analytics cookies

These cookies collect anonymous data about how you use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected. They are set only if you accept cookies.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies in several ways:

  • Consent banner — shown on your first visit. Click "Decline" to reject non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings — you can delete or block cookies in your browser settings at any time.
  • Clear site data — clearing your browser's site data for nomchat-id.up.railway.app will remove all cookies.
⚠️ Note

Deleting the session cookie will sign you out of Nomchat ID.

Third-party cookies

We do not use any third-party cookies or tracking services (e.g. Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel). All cookies are set directly by Nomchat ID.
