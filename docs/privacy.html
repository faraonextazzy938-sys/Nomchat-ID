Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

We collect only what we need: your email, username, and avatar. We never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Email address for authentication
  • Username chosen by you
  • Avatar selected from presets
  • Login timestamps
  • IP address for rate limiting

How we use it

  • To authenticate you across connected apps
  • To display your profile
  • To prevent abuse
  • To send verification codes

Data sharing

We share your username, email, and avatar with apps you authorize via Nomchat ID. We do not sell data to third parties.

Data retention

Data is stored while your account exists. Delete your account anytime from your profile to remove all data permanently.
